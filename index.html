🦠 BugDrug

A conceptual guide to antibiotics, bacteria & clinical syndromes

About this tool & how to use it

Hover (or tap) a syndrome, bacteria, or antibiotic to highlight related items and read notes. Click one or more antibiotics to pin them and see their combined coverage across the bug grid. Use the search boxes to find a specific bacteria (by real species name) or antibiotic. See the Key for what the colors mean.

The antibiotics listed are designed for practice in the United States.

                        The information presented here is designed to be a conceptual guide for learners and is intended for educational purposes only. It is not comprehensive and should not be used for patient care. Some simplifications may not apply in every case, and there is no promise that this information will stay up to date. Reliance on any information provided herein is solely at your own risk.

                        Please consult appropriate reference material or your local ID pharmacist/physician for clinical decision making. Please reach out for any corrections or suggestions.

                        Patient population: Unless stated otherwise, this assumes the patient is an adult who is generally immunocompetent (e.g. not transplant or on immunosuppressants). It also assumes they are not pregnant or breastfeeding.
